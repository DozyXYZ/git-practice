Y'ha-nthlei tharanak ghoi, kadishtu vhlorr grah’n!  
Zog-thuu ai Dagon, uln’ghath f’nilgh'ri.  
Ubb fhthagn v'yah, nyarl zhar'gul uln shol!

Ia! Ia! Dagon wgah’nagl y'hah!

Ngg’gha vulgt’mnah R’lyeh n’gha ygg!  
Il’yagh ftaghu ph’nglui norr'ha!  
K’yarnak tharanak — mog y’hah!  
Uln shagg ygnaii ftagn!

S’uhn Y’ha-nthlei, goka gill’d tharanok.  
Nar’yrrh ulthar ai li’gor lloig!  
Dagon, Zoth-Ommog, ai-yugg!  
Fhalma... fhalma... gnah!

Ia Dagon! Ia Hydra!

Y’ha-nthlei rises from the abyss, sacred and silent!
Praise to Dagon, the Lord Beneath the Salt.
The sleeper waits in the deep, and Nyarlathotep whispers!

Ia! Ia! Dagon dreams in the dark!

The mind drips like seawater from R’lyeh’s wound.
In sleep they speak, in madness they awaken!
The stars turn — and so must we!
The tides know their names.

Within Y’ha-nthlei, the gilled walk undying.
The air above lies, but the ocean chants truth!
Dagon, Zoth-Ommog, rise from the drowned vault!
Return... return... now!

Ia Dagon! Ia Hydra!
